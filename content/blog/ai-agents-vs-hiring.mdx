Every growing business hits the same crossroads: you need more capacity, but hiring is expensive, time-consuming and risky. What if there were a faster way?

## The true cost of hiring

Let's be honest about what it costs to bring someone on board in the UK:

- **Salary**: £30K–£60K for a marketing coordinator or ops assistant
- **Recruitment**: 15–20% of salary via an agency, plus weeks of interviews
- **Onboarding**: 1–3 months before they're fully productive
- **Overhead**: NI, pension, equipment, management time

That's easily **£40K–£80K per year** before they've delivered a single result.

## Where AI agents shine

- **Repetitive, high-volume tasks**: prospecting, email sequences, data entry
- **24/7 operations**: agents work while you sleep
- **Rapid scaling**: add agents in days, not months
- **Consistency**: no human error, no bad days

## Where humans still win

AI agents aren't a replacement for strategic thinking, relationship building or creative leadership. They're best used as a force multiplier — handling the 80% of execution so your team can focus on the 20% that truly needs a human touch.

## The verdict

For most UK SMEs, the answer isn't "AI or people" — it's "AI and fewer, better people." Start with managed AI agents to handle the groundwork, then hire strategically for roles that genuinely need a human.
