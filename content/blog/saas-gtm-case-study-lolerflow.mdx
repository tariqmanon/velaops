Lolerflow is a SaaS product with a small team and big ambitions. The founder was spending over 25 hours a week on go-to-market activities — prospecting, writing emails, creating content, tracking metrics. Something had to change.

## The problem

Like many early-stage SaaS founders, Lolerflow's founder was wearing too many hats. The GTM workload was:

- **Prospecting**: 8 hours/week researching and qualifying leads
- **Outreach**: 6 hours/week writing and sending email sequences
- **Content**: 5 hours/week creating blog posts and social updates
- **Reporting**: 4 hours/week pulling data and building dashboards
- **Monitoring**: 2 hours/week tracking competitors and market trends

Total: **25+ hours per week** on tasks that, while important, were largely repetitive.

## The solution

VelaOps designed a 9-agent network for Lolerflow:

1. **Hunter** — automated prospect research and qualification
2. **Mailer** — personalised email sequences with automatic follow-ups
3. **Sparky** — blog posts and social content in the brand voice
4. **Pulse** — weekly performance dashboards
5. **Tracker** — web analytics and conversion monitoring
6. **Watch** — competitor and market intelligence
7. Plus three supporting agents for data syncing, scheduling and quality assurance

## The results

After two months of optimisation:

- **80% of GTM** now runs autonomously
- The founder spends just **3–4 hours per week** on strategic oversight
- Email open rates increased by **35%** after AI-driven copy improvements
- Content output tripled without any drop in quality

## Key takeaways

1. **Start with your biggest time sinks** — that's where AI agents deliver the fastest ROI
2. **Expect a tuning period** — the first 2–4 weeks involve calibration and optimisation
3. **Think of agents as a team** — they work best as a coordinated network, not isolated tools
