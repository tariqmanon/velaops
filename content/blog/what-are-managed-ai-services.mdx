If you've been hearing about AI agents, large language models and automation but aren't sure where to start, you're not alone. Most business owners know AI could help — they just don't have the time or expertise to figure out how.

That's where **managed AI services** come in.

## What is a managed AI service?

A managed AI service is exactly what it sounds like: a team (or company) that builds, deploys and runs AI tools on your behalf. You get the results — leads, content, reports, automated workflows — without touching a single line of code.

Think of it like hiring a marketing agency, except instead of a team of people doing the work manually, a network of AI agents handles it around the clock.

## Who is it for?

Managed AI services are ideal for:

- **SME founders** who want to scale without scaling headcount
- **Marketing directors** who need more output from a lean team
- **Operations managers** looking to automate repetitive tasks

If your business turns over £500K–£10M and you're spending too much time on tasks that could be automated, managed AI is worth exploring.
